About Me

Hello, I'm Yuvaraj, an artist with 14+ years of experience in fine arts, street arts, drawings, and tattooing. From city streets to canvas, I've explored the vast palette of creativity. As a tattoo artist, I ink stories onto skin, merging the realms of art and personal expression. My journey is fueled by a love for pushing artistic boundaries and creating meaningful connections through visual storytelling. Beyond the studio, I draw inspiration from Dreams, weaving it into my vibrant tapestry of creativity. Let's connect and collaborate – whether you're envisioning a tattoo or exploring the world of art, I'm here to bring your ideas to life.
